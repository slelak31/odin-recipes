Air Fryer Quesadilla Recipe

These air fryer quesadillas are golden and crispy, quick and easy to make, and there are no skillets to wash. Both flour and corn tortillas work for this recipe and the choice of cheese is up to you.

Ingredients

  • 2 flour tortillas
  • 1/2 flour tortillas
  • nonstick cooking spray

Steps

  1. Heat tortillas in the microwave until pliable, about 15 seconds.
  2. Place 1/4 cup cheese on one half of each tortilla; fold the other half of the tortilla over the cheese. Place in the air fryer. Spray with non-stick cooking spray.
  3. Set temperature to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C). Place quesadillas in the basket of the air fryer, and cook until golden brown, 4 to 6 minutes.
  4. Flip quesadillas, press down with a spatula, and air fry for an additional 2 to 3 minutes.
